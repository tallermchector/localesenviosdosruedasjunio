## 5. Components & Patterns
Describes the visual execution of shared UI elements.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Style**: Neo-Brutalist Sharp. Solid backgrounds with thick outlines.
- **Elevation**: High visual impact.
- **Shadow Specs**: `6px 6px 0px 0px #1E3A8A` for Tertiary/Action buttons.
- **Transitions**: Instant snap (-3px translation) to simulate physical switches.

### Cards
- **Background**: Surface (`#F8FAFC`) or White (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Border**: `3px solid #1E3A8A`
- **Shadow**: `8px 8px 0px 0px #1E3A8A`

### Navigation
- **Placement**: Top Navbar, distinct bottom border (`2px solid #1E3A8A`).
- **Behavior**: Sticky. Highly contrasted, solid background.
